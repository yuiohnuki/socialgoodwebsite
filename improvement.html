What is the small steps you can do?

reduce your own waste!!

According to the facts, 50% of our product we bought are being discarded in 6 months. We can easily reduce our waste as much as we can, by considering "Is it necessary?" before your every purchase.

Also, reusing the products, as much as possible also helps to reduce your wastes. Like, donating your clothes or stuffs.

Join the local community's clean ups!

Every community has a cleanup commitment, by jouning so, you will understand how much trash is being discarded in environment. Also, you will able to reduce the waste which eventually reach to the ocean.
