What is the small steps you can do?

reduce your own waste!!

According to the facts, 50% of our product we bought are being discarded within 6 months. We can easily reduce our waste as much as we can by considering "Is it necessary?" before your every purchase.

Also, reusing the products, as much as possible also helps to reduce your wastes. Like, donating your clothes or durable goods.

Join the local community's clean ups!

Every community has a cleanup commitment, by jouning so, you will understand how much trash is being discarded in your environment. Also, you will be able to reduce the waste that eventually reaches the ocean.
